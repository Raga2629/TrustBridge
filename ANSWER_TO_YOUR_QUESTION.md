# ✅ Direct Answer to Your Questions

## Question 1: If newcomer submits any review, will it detect whether it is fake or real?

### Answer: **YES! ✅**

The AI system **automatically analyzes EVERY review** when submitted. It detects:

- ✅ **Genuine reviews** (detailed, specific, natural)
- ⚠️ **Suspicious reviews** (generic, short, questionable)
- ❌ **Fake reviews** (spam, promotional, manipulative)

**Detection happens in real-time** - the moment user clicks "Submit"

---

## Question 2: If detects fake, the review submitted or not?

### Answer: **NO! The fake review is BLOCKED ❌**

When AI detects a fake review:

1. ❌ Review is **NOT saved** to database
2. ❌ Review is **NOT visible** on website
3. ❌ Service rating is **NOT updated**
4. ❌ User sees **error message** explaining why
5. ❌ Review **never appears** anywhere

**Fake reviews are completely rejected and never enter the system.**

## 🛡️ Protection Summary

### What Gets BLOCKED (Never Submitted):
- ❌ Promotional spam: "Call now! Best deal!"
- ❌ Too short: "Good"
- ❌ Excessive emojis: "😍😍😍 Amazing 🎉🎉🎉"
- ❌ Repeated words: "great great great"
- ❌ Copy-paste reviews
- ❌ New account spam (1 day old, 20 reviews)

### What Gets POSTED (Submitted Immediately):
- ✅ Detailed experiences
- ✅ Specific information (price, location, duration)
- ✅ Natural language
- ✅ Balanced feedback
- ✅ Reasonable length (15+ words)

### What Gets FLAGGED (Submitted but Hidden):
- ⚠️ Generic but not spam
- ⚠️ Short but genuine
- ⚠️ First review from new user
- ⚠️ Slightly suspicious patterns
